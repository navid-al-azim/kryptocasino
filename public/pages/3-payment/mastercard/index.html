What Is a Crypto Casino?

An online casino that accepts deposits and withdrawals using cryptocurrencies like Ethereum and Litecoin is known as a crypto casino. Along with standard casino games like slots and table games, it usually provides pure crypto games like Crash X and Aviator.

Different Types of Crypto Casinos

It's important to understand that there are three types of crypto casinos:

  • Pure Bitcoin casinos
  • Crypto casinos that support multiple cryptocurrencies
  • Casinos that offer both cryptocurrency and FIAT options

Pure Bitcoin Casinos

This version was the beginning of it all. In order to circumvent gambling rules, the first cryptocurrency-only casinos existed. In order to cater to the US black market, US casinos such as Bodog made the conversion to Bitcoin.

This is no longer the case; today, serving the demands of the sizable Bitcoin fan base is the main goal of pure Bitcoin casinos. However, since many of them have become cryptocurrency casinos, their numbers have drastically dropped. Furthermore, it seems safer to hang onto your bitcoins and risk with the more unpredictable possibilities.

Crypto Casinos With Multiple Currencies

This increasingly common variant is our favourite. Here, a casino offers cryptos exclusively without FIAT options. Players can choose among several cryptocurrencies. It appears that there are hardly any restrictions, as some casinos provide as many as 20-30 different options.

Advantages of Diversity: Flexibility and Convenience

We are big fans of such options because you can avoid currency exchange and use the cheapest currency or the one you prefer at the moment.

Our Ideal: Deposit With a Wide Crypto Selection

We find it ideal to deposit using a wide range of cryptocurrencies.

Casinos With FIAT and Crypto Options

Casinos offering fiat and crypto options are by far the most widespread. The reason is simple: They want to meet the needs of as many customer groups as possible, and so offer both variants.

Benefits for Crypto Fans and the Role of White Label Casinos

We see that there are no disadvantages to this variant since crypto fans can still use cryptos for payment. Numerous white label casinos are another reason for such constructions.

What Is a White Label Casino?

A white label casino is a turnkey solution that a company can purchase. The operator only needs to take care of the design, support and marketing, and the B2B provider does the rest. It's in the white label providers' interest to cover as many areas as possible so they can better sell their product.

Our Opinion on Casinos Offering FIAT and Crypto Options

We generally find this variant very good as long as it's implemented cleanly and the crypto deposit process is simple and straightforward.

Frequently Asked Questions About Crypto Casinos

What are the advantages of crypto casinos?

Freedom is a significant advantage of crypto casinos as well as cryptocurrencies themselves. In crypto casinos, there are no restrictions regarding game selection or how much you can bet (NOTE: here, self-control is essential) and the state doesn’t follow you at every turn.

What is the best cryptocurrency for a crypto casino?

We recommend you stick to the currency you own and use in your everyday life. Otherwise, you'll have to change back and forth, which can be time-consuming and expensive. However, we can tell you that the two pioneers, BTC and ETH, are also the most popular cryptos of the casinos themselves. Almost all casinos reviewed here offer at least these two well-known currencies.

Which gambling licenses allow the use of cryptocurrencies?

Currently, European licenses don’t allow the use of cryptocurrencies, but the licenses of Curacao and other rather “distant” states allow it.

Can I play table games at a crypto casino?

Yes, you can do that. Table games are not a problem in these casinos. As always, table games contribute little or nothing to the wagering of the welcome bonus or any deposit bonus, but these rules are long known from classic real money casinos.

Are crypto casinos really anonymous?

The question of anonymity cannot be answered very easily. There are different levels of anonymity here. Suppose you choose a pure crypto casino like Stake.com and deposit with a very anonymous method Cardana. In that case, you can even fill out the account using fantasy names and have pretty good anonymity. The counterargument we often hear is that this doesn't speak for trustworthiness, but in the end, protecting one's identity is a valuable fundamental right.

This is why so many payment options have emerged in the crypto scene. If Bitcoin offers solid protection, Monero, in particular, can be used anonymously everywhere.

In which currency a player account at a crypto casino is managed?

A player account can be held in multiple currencies to ensure a clear separation and avoid exchange rate fluctuations.

Are crypto casinos secure?

Compared to pure real money casinos, crypto casinos seem rather unsafe at first, as they are less regulated. However, when choosing a casino, the same rules apply as for “normal casinos.” You should always look for a gambling license, reputable game providers and expert ratings.

How are crypto casinos different from traditional online casinos?

Crypto casinos differ from traditional online casinos mainly in payment methods. While traditional online casinos usually use fiat currencies like Euros or Dollars for deposits and withdrawals, crypto casinos accept cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum or Litecoin.

How can I keep my cryptocurrency safe while using it at a crypto casino?

To ensure the safety of your cryptocurrencies when using them at a crypto casino, follow these steps: use a secure wallet, enable two-factor authentication, watch out for phishing attempts, keep your software up-to-date, and perform regular wallet backups.
